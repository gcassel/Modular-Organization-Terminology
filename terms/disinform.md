#### Definitions
*To disinform* is to *[intentionally](https://github.com/gcassel/Modular-Organization-Terminology/blob/master/terms/intend.md) [misinform](https://github.com/gcassel/Modular-Organization-Terminology/blob/master/terms/misinform.md)*.

*Disinformation* is *intentional misinformation*.

#### Synonyms

*falsehood*, *lie*
